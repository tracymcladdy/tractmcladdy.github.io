At the age of 30

发表于 2017-12-31


I gradually start to read about life, home and emotion recently. I start to care about diet, sleep and health, rather than news, electronics or celebrities. Any negative emotions will be solved rapidly. Instead of being hysteric and vainly attempting to change the world, now I try to refine myself and improve my life.

I learned to do the laundry, cook food, clean the room… I learned to tolerate others, forgive myself, live life mindfully, and become a better person.

I spare no pains to pursue the people or things I love. But I am also aware of my insignificance, narrowness and ignorance.

Life is like a play. After one after another accidents, surprises and singularities, I believe I have finally figured out the role I want to play.
It’s been ten years when I left DUT. I went to HUST, and attained a master’s degree. I did three jobs, and finished several independent projects. I missed a peaceful and beautiful loveI spare no pains to pursue the people or things I love. But I am also aware of my insignificance, narrowness and ignorance. I revere those who are more diligent, intelligent, powerful and successful, those who are far beyond me in every single way.
